Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly can I get my order?

When you order any item on shopExpress, it is meant to be delivered to your address same day to 2 days max, and if you are far from the product location or the product is in a different country like China or the USA. The product is delivered to your address in 7 to 12 days, Max.

How to get a discount on shopExpress?

When you find what you want to buy on shopExpress, call/SMS the customer care line and ask for a discount, they will ask you to order with cash on delivery and provide the order reference Id, and then they apply the discount when they deliver the product to your address.

How to buy Product on shopExpress?
  • Find the product you want on shopExpress App
  • Click "Add to Cart" button near the product
  • At the top right corner, you will see total amount of the iteam
  • Click on the amount you see at the top right, it will take you to your cart
  • Click on Checkout button at the bottom of the cart page
  • Fill the form shown in the Checkout page, and select payment method, and click order button
  • You will get an SMS with your order details and you will be called to confirm your order
Does shopExpress offer free products?

Yes, We provide free products to our customers, but we don't showcase the free product to customers directly, customers find it on the site, you can see free product anywhere on our app. But we don't mention where it is exactly.

Do i need to pay delivery fee for a Free Product?

It depends, on some free products, you will be required to pay a delivery fee, and no delivery fee charged for some of the products on shopExpress.

  • No delivery charge for a free product with 1 to 5kg size
  • Delivery fee is charged when product is higher than 5kg in size
How do I cancel my order?

To cancel your order on shopExpress is to Call, Chat or send an SMS to the shopExpress customer care line that you want to cancel the order, we will ask you for the order reference number. And your order will be canceled without any extra charges.

Why is my account suspended?

If you see your ‘account is inactive or suspended’ when you try logging, it is either your account is newly registered and not yet approved, or you violated a rule that caused the site administrators to suspend your account. If you are convinced you were wrongly suspended or blocked, kindly let us know via the Contact Us page with the details of your account for review. We apologize for any convenience this might have caused you.
